Hello, Welcome to JTMK

JTMK is the Department of Information and Communication Technology offering DDT courses (Diploma in Information Technology (Digital Technology) at Seberang Perai Polytechnic (PSP)).We are dedicated to nurturing a generation of graduates who are not only proficient in information technology but also equipped with critical and innovative thinking. Through our Diploma in Information Technology (Digital Technology) program, students explore the ever-evolving digital landscape, encompassing fields such as software development, data analysis, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.
